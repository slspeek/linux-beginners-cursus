## Instellingen

|Sneltoets|Functie|
|-----------|---------|
|```SUPER+I```|Instellingen|
|```COMPOSE ' e```| é|
|```COMPOSE ` e```| è|
|```COMPOSE " e```| ë|
|```COMPOSE - e```| ē|
|```COMPOSE ^ e```| ê|

Via zoekvak:

|Teken|Naam|
|:---:|:---| 
| é| Acute|
| è| Grave|
| ë | Diaeresis|
| ē | Macron|
| ê | Circumflex|
